Global Park DAO operates on decentralized governance principles.

## Mechanisms

- DAO decisions executed via Snapshot voting
- Treasury managed with multisig (Gnosis Safe)
- Roles for contributors, curators, and delegates

## Phases

1. **Genesis**: initial curation and onboarding: construct roles, first votes, testing tools

2. **Growth**: broader participation, funding decisions

3. **Stability**: refined structure, regular timeline, automated governance cycles

## Governance Tools

- **Snapshot**: off-chain proposals & voting
- **Gnosis Safe**: multisig treasury control
- **Docs**: transparent publication of grovernance rules & records
