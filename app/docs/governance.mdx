# Governance & DAO Structure

The GlobalPark DAO ensures decentralized and transparent decision-making across the ecosystem.

## Principles

- **On-chain voting:** Decisions are made by token holders
- **Proposal system:** Any member can submit ideas for improvement
- **Treasury management:** Community-controlled fund allocation

We believe in open participation and community-led innovation.
