Global Park DAO | Vision, Mission, Ethos

We're building a decentralized, community-driven initiative focused on culture, arts, technology, and collective memory.

## Values

- Decentralized public governance
- Cultural heritage as a public good
- Token-based participation over speculation
- Phygital Memory and Open Representation

## Mission

The mission of Global Park DAO is to build an interactive, semi-eternal, and culturally significant platform for preserving digital and physical heritage.

By blending Web3 tools, onchain data, and physical spaces, we aim to activate public communities, empower civic engagement, secure memorial artifacts, and bring meaningful access to cultural values.
