# ℘ GPark Overview

Global Park DAO | Vision, Mission, Ethos

We’re building a decentralized, community-driven initiative focused on culture, arts, and technology.

**Values***

- Decentralization & public governance
- Cultural heritage as a public good
- Token-based participation, not speculation
- Phygital Memory & Open Representation

**Mission**

The mission of Global Park DAO is to build an interactive, semi-eternal, and culturally significant platform for preserving digital and physical heritage.

By blending web3 tools, onchain data, and physical spaces, we aim to activate public communities, empower civic engagement, and create a new infrastrucure of memory.
