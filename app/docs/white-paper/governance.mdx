# Governance Structure

The governance of Global Park DAO is founded on transparency, participation, and memory-layered structure.

At its core, the system uses a three-tiered governance model:

- **Strategic Consultative Board**
- **Elected Representatives (bow agents)**
- **Communities with voting rights**
